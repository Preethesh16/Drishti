Process

How KumbhDoot Works

Designed to work even in low connectivity. Offline-first, voice-first, stress-tested for 80 million users.

1
Register Your Group

Create a family pod before you arrive. Add every member by name, photo, and phone number — including those without smartphones.

2
Go Live at Kumbh

The app activates at Prayagraj. Real-time location sharing starts automatically. All data is encrypted and private.

3
If Separated, Speak

Open KumbhDoot and say the lost person's name and description. AI matches against the live database in under 3 seconds.

4
Reunite

A volunteer or family member gets the location. Meet at the nearest designated reunion point. Simple, fast, sacred.
